Hamburger

What is a hamburger?

A hamburger is a popular sandwich consisting of a ground beef patty, typically grilled or fried, served inside a soft bun. It can be customized with various toppings such as lettuce, tomato, cheese, pickles, and condiments like ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise.

Hamburgers are often paired with side items like fries or onion rings and are a staple in many fast-food chains and casual restaurants. Variations of the classic burger can include different types of meat or vegetarian options, offering something for every taste.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Seasonings
  • Herbs
  • Sauces

Steps

  1. Combine the ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Make four patties.
  3. Grill the patties untill the juices run clear and the meat is no longer pink in the center.
